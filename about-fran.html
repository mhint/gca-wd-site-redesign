Meet Francine "Fran" Miller

Fran's Flowers began with the vision of our founder, Francine “Fran” Miller, who opened the shop in 1995 with a simple mission: to share joy, beauty, and connection through flowers. Inspired by the vibrant heart of San Francisco's Mission District, Fran built a place where neighbors could gather, celebrate, and find comfort in nature's colors.

Three decades later, her spirit still guides every bouquet we create. Fran's dedication to community remains at the root of our work — with ten percent of every sale supporting local efforts to fight homelessness across the Bay Area.

Whether you've been a customer since the early days or are visiting for the first time, you'll feel Fran's touch in every arrangement: thoughtful, generous, and full of life.
